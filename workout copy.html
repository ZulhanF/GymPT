🟢 LEVEL 1 – PEMULA (Beginner)

Durasi: 4–6 minggu
Fokus: Adaptasi tubuh, membentuk kebiasaan, teknik dasar

Hari 1: Full Body

  • Push-up (dengan lutut) – 3x10
  • Squat (berat badan) – 3x15
  • Plank – 3x20 detik
  • Dumbbell Row – 3x10 per sisi

Hari 2: Istirahat

Hari 3: Kardio + Core

  • Jalan cepat/treadmill – 20 menit
  • Crunch – 3x15
  • Bicycle crunch – 3x15
  • Leg raise – 3x12

Hari 4: Istirahat

Hari 5: Full Body 2

  • Dumbbell Chest Press – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Lunges – 3x12 per kaki
  • Plank – 3x30 detik

Hari 6 & 7: Istirahat / Jalan santai

🟡 LEVEL 2 – MENENGAH (Intermediate)

Durasi: 4–6 minggu
Fokus: Peningkatan kekuatan dan daya tahan

Hari 1: Push Day (Dada, Bahu, Triceps)

  • Bench Press – 4x8
  • Shoulder Press – 3x10
  • Dumbbell Fly – 3x12
  • Triceps Dips – 3x12

Hari 2: Pull Day (Punggung, Biceps)

  • Lat Pulldown – 4x10
  • Seated Row – 3x12
  • Barbell Curl – 3x12
  • Hammer Curl – 3x10

Hari 3: Leg Day

  • Squat – 4x8
  • Leg Press – 3x12
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3x10
  • Calf Raise – 3x15

Hari 4: Istirahat Aktif (yoga/kardio ringan)

Hari 5: Full Body

  • Deadlift – 4x6
  • Pull-up (dibantu kalau perlu) – 3x8
  • Dumbbell Lunge – 3x10 per kaki
  • Plank – 3x45 detik

Hari 6–7: Kardio opsional / Istirahat

🔴 LEVEL 3 – LANJUTAN (Advanced)

Durasi: Berkelanjutan
Fokus: Hypertrophy, kekuatan maksimal, estetika

Hari 1: Chest & Triceps

  • Barbell Bench Press – 5x5
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x8
  • Chest Fly Machine – 3x12
  • Skull Crusher – 3x10

Hari 2: Back & Biceps

  • Deadlift – 5x5
  • Pull-up – 4x10
  • Barbell Row – 4x8
  • Concentration Curl – 3x10

Hari 3: Legs & Abs

  • Barbell Squat – 5x5
  • Leg Curl – 4x12
  • Walking Lunges – 3x20 langkah
  • Hanging Leg Raise – 3x15

Hari 4: Shoulders & Traps

  • Military Press – 4x8
  • Lateral Raise – 3x15
  • Front Raise – 3x12
  • Barbell Shrug – 4x10

Hari 5: Full Body Circuit

  • Kettlebell Swing – 3x20
  • Burpees – 3x15
  • Box Jump – 3x12
  • Russian Twist – 3x20

Hari 6–7: Recovery / Aktif stretching
